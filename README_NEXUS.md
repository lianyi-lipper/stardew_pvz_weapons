# Electric Gatling Pea - PvZ Weapon for Stardew Valley

Bring the firepower of **Plants vs Zombies 2** into your Stardew Valley farm! This mod adds the Electric Gatling Pea as a powerful craftable slingshot weapon.

---

## Features

### ⚡ Electric Gatling Pea

A devastating electric-powered slingshot inspired by PvZ2's Gatling Pea:

- **Quad Shot**: Fires 4 piercing electric bullets per attack
- **Chain Lightning**: Electric arcs jump between nearby enemies
- **Destructive Mode**: Press **V** to toggle - destroys trees, rocks, and obstacles
- **Ultimate Ability**: 30% chance to trigger Electric Burst - rapid fires 210 bullets over 1.5 seconds!
- **Piercing Projectiles**: Bullets pass through multiple enemies and obstacles

### Damage Stats
| Attack Type | Damage | Notes |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| Normal Shot | 50 per bullet | Stuns enemies briefly |
| Chain Lightning | 25 | Arcs to nearby enemies |
| Ultimate Burst | 50 x 210 bullets | Massive area damage |

---

## Installation

1. Install [SMAPI](https://smapi.io/) (4.0.0 or later)
2. Download this mod
3. Extract to your `Stardew Valley/Mods/` folder
4. Launch the game!

---

## How to Obtain

### Crafting Recipe
Craft at any crafting station once you have the materials:

| Material | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Battery Pack | 5 |
| Iridium Bar | 10 |

### Console Command (for testing)
Open SMAPI console (~) and type:
```
player_add (W)lianyi-lipper.StardewPvZWeapons_ElectricGatlingPea
```

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Left Click | Fire (hold for rapid fire) |
| Right Click | Ultimate Attack |
| V | Toggle Destructive Mode |
